What the GENIUS Act is
A U.S. federal framework for regulating payment stablecoins and their issuers.
Why it matters
Compliance, reserves, consumer protection, and clearer rules for market participation.
What this page is
An independent information + contact page for marketing campaigns (official portal).

Simple explanation

The GENIUS Act (S. 1582) created a federal approach to overseeing payment stablecoins. It requires significant implementation work by agencies through rulemaking and application processes.

Core idea
More clarity + guardrails for stablecoin issuance and oversight.
Implementation
Agencies are issuing proposals and guidance to execute the law.
Debate
Supporters cite safeguards; critics cite gaps and systemic risks.
